I can not find a player

We only find out about players when they get searched for or via a friend already in our database.

A player exists but does not have a rank

Ranks are not live and get calculated at midnight (GMT), so new players may not have a rank right away.
We may not have access to your stats, you can allow access at by editing your profile on steamcommunity.com and clicking "My Privacy Settings".

How can I help out?

How do we gather user data?

Steam has dozens of APIs we can call to get data from them. Everything on Game DB is also availible using the steam app.
